Whenever you visit our website and make a booking you are trusting us with your personal information.

The personal information we collect from you for this includes your name and contact details.

This personal information is collected in order to allow you to make restaurant bookings, and is gathered at the time of the booking.

Providing some information is optional. If you choose not to enter your name and contact details, we will be unable to complete a restaurant booking for you.

We take this responsibility, and your privacy seriously, by ensuring we comply with the New Zealand Privacy Act 2020 when handling your personal information.

We do not share any information with third-parties regarding individuals.

You have the right to ask for a copy of any personal information we hold about you, and to ask for it to be corrected if you think it is wrong.
If you would like to ask for a copy of your information or have the information corrected, or if you have any questions about our site or our privacy policy,
